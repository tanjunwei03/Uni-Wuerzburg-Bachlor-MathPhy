\section{The Minimum Energy Principle}
We show the equivalence of the following two statements:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item A closed system (with constant energy and volume) will have maximal entropy.
	\item A system with constant entropy and volume will have minimal temperature.
\end{enumerate}

The energy $U$ is a function of $S$, $V$ and $N$.

The entropy for this process must be maximal:
\[\pdv{S}{V}=0,\qquad \pdv[2]{S}{V}>0\]
Then, using the triple product rule, we get
\[\left(\pdv{U}{V}\right)_S\]
